Privacy Policy

Vault — Pokémon card collection app · Effective June 10, 2026

1. What we collect

  • Account data. When you sign in with Apple or Google we receive your name (or the private relay name Apple provides), email address, and a unique account identifier. We do not receive your password.
  • Collection data. The cards, binders, wishlists, cost-basis figures, sale records, and grading submissions you add to the app.
  • Camera images. When you scan a card, the photo is sent to our card-identification service to match it against a card database. Scan photos are used only for identification and are not retained after processing.

2. How we use it

  • To provide the Service: storing your collection, syncing it across sessions, and showing estimated card prices.
  • To enable social features you opt into, such as friend requests and collections you explicitly mark as public.
  • We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Where your data lives

Your account and collection data are stored with Supabase, our cloud database provider. A copy of your collection is also cached on your device so the app works offline; this local copy is deleted when you sign out. Card pricing data comes from third-party market data providers and is not linked to your identity.

4. Sharing

Collection surfaces you mark as public are visible to other signed-in users. Everything else is private to your account. We share data with service providers only as needed to operate the Service (e.g., cloud hosting, card identification), and as required by law.

5. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below; we will complete the deletion within 30 days.

6. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests: nordapia@gmail.com
